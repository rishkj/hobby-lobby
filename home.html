Welcome to Hobby Lobby-The ultimate extra curriculum hub. Here at Hobby Lobby, we encourage youngsters to explore and build their creative side. We provide a platform to encourage our students to take a stand on their hobbies. This builds their confidence and is a push to their overall personilty development.

Hobby Lobby was a vision that was established on 5th November, 1995 in Bangalore. It was initiated by Jeevana Hegde and Rishbah Jain, two renown artists. Hobby Lobby was a small franchise that had to undergo major turns as "arts" was not a widely supported and believed idea back in that period. However, it took a leap forward as it had a turn-out that was more than expected. This helped Hobby Lobby grow and extend to other cities across India including Delhi and Mumbai.

Rishabh Jain
Rishabh Jain is a trained martial artist and athlete. He has been a part of the prestigious Indian Combat Sports Academy and Indian Athletic Academy. Not only has he undergone vigorous training in these two feilds but he is also a man with great interest and taste in Music. We are honoured to run the institution under his guidance.

Jeevana Hegde
Jeevana Hegde is an exceptional classical singer and dancer who has been recognised for her enormous achievement in the field of art and culture. She began her career at a young age of 4 under Shri. Hamsalekha sir and Ms. Jayalakshmi Jitendra. She has taken part in several television programs both nationally and internationally. We are grateful to be running this institution in her guidance.

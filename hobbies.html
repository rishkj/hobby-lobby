            Hobbies

  1. Dance
    • Western Dance:

      We offer a wide range of western dance forms which inlcude salsa, cha cha cha, ballet, afro and a couple of street styles like crump, lockin' and poppin' and b-boyin'. Our students not only learn but have a fun time with one bollywood dance class a week.

    • Classical Dance:

      Keeping up with the tradition and encouraging the newer generations to understand and respect Indian classical danceforms is a necessacity. We offer north as well as south Indian classical dance forms like kathak and bharatnatyam.

  2. Sports
    • Basket ball:

      The team sport is trained with at most professionalism. The idea of free spirit is uplheld and team spirit brings the youngsters together for a spinning face-off.

    • Cricket:

      We don't want a single Sachin amongst the lads. We're here to build the entire National team. Join the hood!

    • Badminton:

      Strategy, power and body control are our key features. We keep any other emotion outside the court and make those shots worth watching.

    • Swimming:

      Your time on vacations are only going to get more relaxing with some cool time underwater. Take it as a hobby or porfessionally, we are here to guide you personally. We offer freestyle, back stroke, butterfly stroke and floating.

  3. Music
    • Vocal:

      Music is the easiest way of time travel. Here we are to help you guys turn from bathroom singers to play back artists or those superstars you listen to all day.

    • Guitar:

      As soothing as it is to hear, it seeps in our minds and gets into our hearts. Be that respective sense of attraction at any event by joining the guitar classes

    • Keyboard:

      Magic is no more in the air, it lies within and can reach us via your fingers. It's no more just "Happy Birthday" tunes. Get ready to play the sounds of your souls!

  4. Karate

    Self defense is no more just a hobby, it's a necessacity. Help your kids build their grow strong enough to help themselves and others. Our porfessional martial artists are here for you'll.

  5. Painting

    What runs in your mind can be told so beautifully with your brushes. Let your mind relax and heart do the job once you join our painting course. It's time we embrace the imperfect creativity within us.

  6. Theatre

    A daily dose of drama ain't hurt nobody ;) Settle in and prep up yourselves to be a part of a crazy journey of a 100 tales and so many faces. Join the drama club.
